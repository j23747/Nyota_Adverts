General Questions

Q. How do I join?

A. Go to the sign up tab and create an account choosing to be an influencer or a brand.

Q. Do you require an ID to register?

A. Yes you are required a National Identification card to verify your identity

Q. How does the company benefit?

A. When a brand and an influencer work together , the company gets a certain percentage depending on the contract signed by all parties

Q. How much do you charge?

A. Registration is free but if involved in a gig as an influencer or a Brand , the company will charge a certain percentage depending on the type of campaign being done

Q. Do you design the advert videos/posters?

A. We have a talented team of designers who design the posters/videos charging a smaal fee.However we accept suggestions from both parties on how they would love their videos/posters to look like

Q. How sure am I that I will be paid after a gig?

A. Before the campaign begins , both parties sign a legal contract that binds the agreement
